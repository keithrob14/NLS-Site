Native Landscapes is a land, water, and wildlife habitat management company located in Ohio. Native Landscapes was founded in 2018 with a desire to help landowners manage their landscapes in a natural and sustainable manner. In doing so, we restore native ecosystems, create beautiful and productive landscapes, promote habitat for all types of wildlife species. We assist landowners in accomplishing their property goals while working in harmony with the natural world. Landscapes that restore the balances found in native settings are our specialty at Native Landscapes and we can design a project to help you whether you're working with an urban backyard or a thousand-acre property. Education remains our top priority and we would love to hear from you if you have a landscape you would like professional help managing. Our site is currently under maintenance, we apologize for any difficulties.
